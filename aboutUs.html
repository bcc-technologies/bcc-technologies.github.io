About Us

Who are we? We are BCC Tech, a passionate group of programmer-scientists dedicated to exploring the limits of technology and innovation. Our story begins as physics students with a deep curiosity for the unknown and a passion for expanding knowledge.

Our name, in addition to combining our last names, is a tribute to the "Body Central Cubic" which is a concept of importance in solid-state physics.

What do we do? At BCC Tech, we specialize in custom software development that transforms ideas into concrete solutions. Whether creating cross-platform applications for companies in various industries or designing innovative products for the general public, our approach is always the same: make the complex easy and expand the interesting.
